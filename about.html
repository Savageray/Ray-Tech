About RayTech Foundation

RayTech Foundation is a forward-thinking technology solutions provider based in Nairobi, Kenya. Founded in 2025 by Ryan Jesse Wanyonyi, we deliver innovative, reliable, and affordable digital services tailored to the needs of small and medium businesses.
